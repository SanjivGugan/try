Jperm AKA Dylan Q. Wang
Dylan Q. Wang, better known online as J Perm, is a Canadian cubing YouTuber known for his Rubik's Cube related videos. He holds two national records (tied FMC single + FMC average.) Dylan is sponsored by the Cube Retailer SpeedCubeShop. Dylan Q. Wang was born in Richmond, British Columbia. He started to play tennis at 11 years old. He attended the University of British Columbia, where he studied computer science and played tennis. When he was 10 years old he got his first cube as a gift for Christmas


Max Park
Max Park is an American Rubik's Cube speedsolver of Korean descent who formerly held the world record average of five 3x3x3 solves (by WCA traditions), 6.39 seconds, set on 23 April 2017 at OCSEF Open 2017. Prior to this, the record had been held by Feliks Zemdegs of Australia, who had improved it 9 times over 7 years from 9.21 seconds on 30 January 2010 to 6.45 seconds. Park was previously the only cuber other than Feliks Zemdegs to have set the record since 27 September 2009 until Ruihang Xu broke it on 5 June 2021. He has also set multiple world records in solving the 4x4x4, 5x5x5, 6x6x6, and 7x7x7 cubes, and 3x3x3 one-handed. He has won 297 events across many Rubik's cube competitions.


Feliks Zemdegs
Feliks Aleksanders Zemdegs born 20 December 1995) is an Australian Rubik's Cube speedsolver. He is the only speedcuber to ever win the World Cube Association World Championship twice, winning in 2013 and 2015, and has set more than 100 world records across various speedcubing events. He is often considered the greatest speedsolver of all time.
